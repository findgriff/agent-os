Construction

"AGENT" is set in Space Grotesk 600 with +16% tracking — engineered, evenly-spaced, mission-patch calm. "OS" steps up in size and weight (700) so the product name lands on the platform, not the adjective.

The Orbital O

The "O" is a planetary body drawn as a pure ring; a 24°-inclined orbit sweeps around it, carrying a single satellite node — one agent, on-mission. The orbit shifts cyan → violet, the brand's energy gradient.

Behaviour

On dark surfaces only. The mark carries a soft cyan bloom (drop-shadow 0 0 24px); never boxed, never outlined. At <24px, drop the satellite node and keep ring + O.
